**Description:**
```
You're holding a product at the store, reading the ingredients list: E471, sodium benzoate, modified starch... You can read it, but what does it actually mean? If your child has an allergy, your partner has a dietary restriction, or you simply care about what you eat, that label is hard to decode.

PureScan Foods is built to decode it. It is a food ingredient scanner that analyzes the E-codes and additives on food labels.

HOW IT WORKS
Three ways to scan:
• Camera (OCR): Snap a photo of the label and the ingredients list is read and analyzed automatically
• Barcode: Product data is pulled from the OpenFoodFacts database
• Text input: Paste an ingredients list

ADDITIVE & E-CODE ANALYSIS
Every scan breaks additives down by risk class. The EU regulatory status of E-codes, the NOVA food-processing level, and the Nutri-Score are shown on a single screen.

FAMILY PROFILES & ALLERGEN TRACKING
Automatic warnings for 23 allergens including gluten, lactose, peanut, egg and milk. Define a separate diet profile and allergen sensitivity for each family member. When you scan a product, compatibility for all profiles is calculated at once.

GURU: AI NUTRITION ASSISTANT
Guru knows your family profiles and scan history. Ask "What is E249?" or "Is this product suitable for a vegan diet?" and get answers tailored to your profile.

ADDITIVES ENCYCLOPEDIA
150+ food additives with E-code, EU legal status, risk class (dangerous, caution, safe) and plain-language explanations. A quick reference for when an E-code stumps you at the store.

20 DIET PROFILES
Vegan, vegetarian, ketogenic, gluten-free, lactose-free, halal, kosher, FODMAP, Mediterranean, pregnancy and breastfeeding, and more. The EU's 14 mandatory allergens plus 9 additional allergens are flagged automatically on every scan.

SCAN HISTORY
All your scans are saved. Return to a product with one tap, or add a past scan to a Guru conversation as context.

PureScan Foods does not provide medical advice or dietary prescriptions. It is a content-analysis tool that digitizes the information on food labels; the consumption decision is yours.
```
